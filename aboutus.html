OUR STORY

Discover the passion, culture, and community behind Plate of Africa

WHERE IT ALL STARTED

When the founders decided to create Plate of Africa, they envisioned it as more than just a restaurant. Plate of Africa is a place where people of all cultures, and all backgrounds, can come together under the shared passion for culinary excellence.

Plate of Africa was created simply by three men, each with a deep passion for cooking. Whether it be a medium rare steak, or a perfectly baked focaccia, these men were absolutely enthralled with the complexity of food. They found that food was not just about flavor or texture, but truly about the people you share the food with.

This passion led to these men deciding to pursue a culinary education at Auguste Escoffier School of Culinary Arts in Austin, Texas, earning a diploma in Plant-Based Culinary Arts. After graduating, they began to work as chefs at La Lumiere Etoilee, their first professional career.

However, despite their job stability and skills, they felt a sense of emptiness. The food that they cooked always seemed to be missing something essential. As they traveled around the world, they realized the essential thing they were missing was that of great simplicity: Community.

WHAT MAKES US SPECIAL

Authentic Cuisine

Our dishes are not from just one area or region, but the entire continent of Africa. We bring you the richness of West African stews and the complex spices of East African cuisine.

Plant-Based Focus

We've reimagined traditional African dishes through a plant-based lens, creating sustainable and delicious meals that honor both tradition and innovation.

Community Spirit

What truly makes Plate of Africa different is the community it creates through food. Every dish is a gateway to a different world and culture.

OUR MISSION

Our mission at Plate of Africa is to foster community through the flavors of Africa using a plant-based lens. We hope that through the power of plant-based cooking, we can create a community that shares the same values of community.

Every dish that we serve to our community is put together using love, sourced locally ingredients, and created with sustainability in mind. We hope that by using plant-based cooking methods, we can create dishes that not only nourish the body, but also the soul.

OUR COMMUNITY

Inclusive Space

Community drives everything we do here at Plate of Africa. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat, it is a space where people come together to share food, stories, and connections.

Giving Back

Plate of Africa works proudly alongside a charity called African Horizons. 7% of all revenue generated goes directly towards supporting education, healthcare, and food security across underserved regions in Africa.

Creating Impact

By dining with us, you are not only enjoying authentic African deliciousness — but you are also helping to build pathways for a better and sustainable future in communities that need all the help they can get.

At Plate of Africa, we believe that nothing unites people like great food, and as a collective, we can create an impact that extends far beyond our dining room.
