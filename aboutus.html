ABOUT US

At Plate of Africa, we’re more than a restaurant—we’re a celebration of heritage, flavor, and community. Discover how our passionate chefs turned a love for African cuisine into a mission to redefine vegetarian-based dining, one shared meal at a time.

WHERE IT ALL STARTED

When we decided to create Plate of Africa, we envisioned it as more than just a restaurant. Plate of Africa is a place where people of all cultures, and all backgrounds, can come together under the shared passion for culinary excellence. Plate of Africa was created simply by three men, each with a passion for cooking. Whether it be a flavorful chickpea curry, or a perfectly baked focaccia, these men were absolutely enthralled with the complexity of food. They found that food was not just about flavor or texture, but about the people you share the food with.


This passion led to these men deciding to pursue a culinary education at Auguste Escoffier School of Culinary Arts in Austin, Texas, earning a diploma in Plant-Based Culinary Arts. After graduating, they began to work as chefs at La Lumiere Etoilee, their first professional career. However, despite their job stability, and skills, they felt a sense of emptiness. The food that they cooked always seemed to be missing something essential. As they traveled, they realized the essential thing they were missing was that of great simplicity: Community.


This realization drove them to create Plate of Africa, a place where people could combine culinary excellence and community can combine to bring an amazing experience. They decided to choose to specialize in African cuisine as it was the food they had been eating all of their lives. Specifically, they decided that a vegan restaurant would be the best, due to its many environmental and health benefits. This was the beginning of an idea that created the Plate of Africa you see today.


As Plate of Africa begins to move towards the future, we will continue to uphold the core values that founded us: that of culinary excellence, passion, and most of all, community. We are beyond thrilled to share with you our passion, and to create lasting memories one plate at a time.

Our Core Values

Cultural Authenticity

We honor the rich culinary traditions of the African continent, preserving authentic flavors and cooking techniques while innovating for today's diners.

Environmental Stewardship

Our plant-based approach significantly reduces environmental impact. We strive to minimize waste, conserve water, and source ingredients sustainably.

Community Connection

We create spaces where people can connect through food, celebrating the communal aspect of dining that is central to African culture.

WHAT MAKES US SPECIAL

What sets Plate of Africa apart from other restaurants is the diversity we bring to the table. The dishes served here are not from just one area or one region, but the entire continent. Whether it be the richness from West Africa stews, to the complex spices used in East African cuisine, our dishes encompass all different types of flavors, textures, and cooking methods. Our chef's are committed to serving only the most authentic cuisine, using only the freshest ingredients. The recipes that make our dishes are recipes that have been used for generations, perfected and refined by each generation before.


However, the thing that truly makes the Plate of Africa different is the community it creates through its food. Every dish is not just food, but a gateway towards a whole different world that you may not be familiar with. It is a way to explore different cultures, and create connections through the shared values of food. Our diversity is not simply limited to our menu, but extends to the environment we create.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission at Plate of Africa is to foster community through the flavors of Africa using a plant-based lens. We hope that through the power of plant-based cooking, we can create a community that shares the same values of community. Every dish that we serve to our community is put together using love, source locally, and put together with sustainability in mind. We hope that through using plant-based cooking methods, we can create dishes that not only nourish the body, but also the soul.

OUR COMMUNITY

Community drives everything we do here at Plate of Africa. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat, it is a space where people come together to share food, stories, and connection. We are dedicated to cultivating an inclusive community, a friendly and welcoming space where everyone feels at home; we're into establishing relationships that go beyond the meal.


To help give back, Plate of Africa works proudly alongside a charity called African Horizons. African Horizons aims to assist the less fortunate across Africa. African Horizons strives to deliver essential services such as education, healthcare, and food security across underserved regions. In order to assist their global mission, that is where 7% of all revenue generated goes directly towards supporting that impact we want to create.


By dining with us, you are not only enjoying authentic African deliciousness — but you are also helping to build pathways for a better and sustainable future in communities that need all the help they can get. We at Plate of Africa believe that nothing unites people like great food, and as a collective, we can create an impact.
