Are you interested in working for Plate of Africa? We are always looking for new, motivated minds to contribute to our family. Whether it be the front or the back of the house, we want you to look into joining our family.
Why Join?
Join an Inclusive Community: As a Plate of Africa employee, you're not just an employee, but a member of our family.
Competitive Salary: We believe that workers should be fairly compensated for their labour. Plate of Africa is among the top 10% of restaurants in average employee salary.
Make an Impact: Join us on our mission to foster community through an environmentally-friendly dining experience.
Requirements
Strong work ethic and willpower
Ability to work a minimum of three days per week
Must have a minimum of two years of work experience in fine dining
Food Handler's License (Required for both front and back of the house staff)
