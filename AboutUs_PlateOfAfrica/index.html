    About


    PLATE OF AFRICA


    Plate of Africa is an African restaurant that specializes in the diverse cuisines found throughout the African continent, through an entirely plant-based menu. We celebrate the culinary history found throughout Africa, recrafted into modern, health-conscious dining. Our dishes can be found throughout the continent, and include North, South, East, and West African cuisine.


    We commit ourselves to a high standard of culinary excellence, and invite you to experience the vast flavors that Africa has to offer. Many ingredients used in the creation of our dishes are sourced locally, prioritizing excellence without compromise.

    Our


    Mission


    Our Mission at Plate of Africa is to foster community through the flavors of Africa using a plant-based lens. We hope that through the power of plant-based cooking, we can create a community that shares the same values of community. Every dish that we serve to our community is put together using love, source locally, and put together with sustainability in mind. We hope that through using plant-based cooking methods, we can create dishes that not only nourish the body, but also the soul.

    • Showcasting cuisines from North, South, East, and West Africa.

    • Prioritizing a vegan and vegetarian menu.

    • Source locally and sustainability.

    • Maintaining traditional recipes with a vegan twist.
