About
Plate of Africa
We celebrate the culinary history found throughout Africa, by recrafting it into modern, health-conscious dining. Our dishes can be found throughout the continent, and includes North, South, East, and West African cuisine.
We commit ourselves to a high standard of culinary excellence, and invite you to experience the vast flavors that Africa has to offer. Many ingredients used in the creation of our dishes are directly shipped from trusted local farmers to ensure quality ingredients, prioritizing excellence without compromise.

Why

Veganism?

Plate of Africa was founded to celebrate Africa’s rich culinary heritage through a modern, plant-based approach. A multitude of African dishes are naturally vegan, making it the perfect foundation for a menu that stays true to tradition.
